Cookies

When a user visits Art-Geek-Design, there are certain files that are downloaded onto your computer which allows you to view the website. Along with these files a short text file, called a “cookie”, is downloaded to your computer as long as your settings allow it. This information is harmless, and used to help improve the speed and service of Art-Geek-Design.com. A cookie is a small text file of alpha-numeric characters that is used to identify your computer browser. It stores small amounts of information, including visitors’ preferences, records user-specific information on which pages the site visitor accesses or visits, and personalizes or customizes our web page content based upon visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. This is done primarily to hopefully that improve navigation and user experience. ### Disabling Cookies Although it is not recommended that our visitors disable the browser’s ability to accept cookies, it is understood that a visitor may feel more comfortable doing so for their own personal security. In this regard we offer you the following information here. Please feel free to search our site for further information (tutorials) on other aspects of securing your browsing experience. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites. [What Are Cookies?](http://www.privacypolicyonline.com/what-are-cookies)

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, Art-Geek-Design makes use of log files. These files merely log a users visit to the site and how they interact with the website in question. This is generally a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services’s analytics. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks a user makes while visiting the site. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. This information helps drakenhart-studios.net to improve and streamline a visitor’s interaction, and hopefully improve both information flow and sales by providing visitors with the best possible user experience we are able to provide.
